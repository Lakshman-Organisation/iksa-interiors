Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with IKSA Architects.

Interior design costs vary based on scope, materials, and complexity. At IKSA Architects, we offer customized packages starting from budget-friendly options to luxury premium designs. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your space and requirements.

Project timelines depend on the size and scope. A typical residential project takes 45-90 days from design approval to handover. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.
